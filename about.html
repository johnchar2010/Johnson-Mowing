About Us

At Johnson Mowing, we believe a well-manicured lawn is the heartbeat of a happy home. Based right here in the heart of Wisconsin, we specialize in professional lawnmowing services designed to keep your property looking sharp through every season the Midwest throws our way. From the first spring green-up to the final fall cleanup, our team is committed to providing reliable, high-quality care that goes beyond just "cutting the grass."

“Hola, I’m Martino, and I’ve been working in lawn care for years because I truly enjoy hard work and making customers happy. Growing up, I learned the value of showing up early, working hard, and taking pride in every job no matter how big or small. At Johnson Mowing, I love being part of a team that treats customers like family and always gives 100% on every lawn we service. Seeing a yard transformed after a long day’s work is what motivates me, and I’m proud to help make neighborhoods look their best.”
